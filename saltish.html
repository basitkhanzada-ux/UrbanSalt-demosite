Urban Salt Kitchen & Bar is a casual eatery and full-service cocktail bar located in the heart of downtown San Angelo. We serve creative American fare with global influences, handcrafted cocktails, and daily specials.

Co-owned by Michelle Chapa and Chef Ronnie Cajas, our space is designed for unforgettable evenings — whether you’re joining us for weekend brunch or winding down with friends over dinner and premium drinks.
